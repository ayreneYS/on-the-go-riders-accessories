About Us

On The Go Back Strap Phone Holder is designed for motorcycle riders and back-ride passengers who find it difficult to hold and secure their phone while on a ride, navigating the route, and those who fear the chances of their phones being snatched by strangers, falling, and being damaged by rain.

Our On the Go Back Strap Phone Holder is an accessory designed for long rides and travelers that provides comfort as well as the safety, durability, and protection of their phones while traveling. That further provides a hands-free and hassle-free ride for the passenger while still being able to view and navigate their routes.

On The Go Back Strap Phone Holder will secure the phones of the driver and passenger of the motorcycle. It has a solution that turns hands-free and hassle-free, which provides comfort as well as the safety, durability, and protection of their phones while on a ride. This allows our customers to still use and view their phones safely even when it's raining because of its waterproof case.

We also promote safety in all seasons because our product will protect you day and night with the reflective strap, rainy or sunny, with the waterproof case, and with the durability of the holder.
